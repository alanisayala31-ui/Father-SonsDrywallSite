Our Recent Projects

At Father & Sons Drywall, we take pride in our craftsmanship and attention to detail. Each project showcases our commitment to high-quality work, ensuring that every drywall installation is seamless and visually appealing. Our team works diligently to maintain a clean job site, respecting your space as if it were our own.

One of our recent residential projects involved transforming a dated living room into a modern, inviting space. We meticulously hung drywall, ensuring smooth finishes and perfect alignment, which resulted in a stunning transformation that delighted our clients.

In a commercial setting, we completed a large office renovation where precision was key. Our team efficiently installed drywall throughout the space, adhering to strict timelines while maintaining our high standards. The result was a professional environment that met the client's needs and exceeded their expectations.

We also specialize in repair work, addressing issues like water damage or wear and tear. Our careful approach to repairs not only restores the integrity of the walls but also enhances the overall aesthetic of the space.

Every project we undertake reflects our family values of trust and quality. We believe that a job well done is the foundation of our business, and we are proud to showcase our work in the Twin Cities Metro Area.
